Birthday-app

Birthday-app is an app that tells you when you were born and your name in Ghana/Akan name.Both males and females from any country and continent can find out their Akan names using the app

How to use the app:

  • Enter the day you were born in the first field
  • Enter the day you were born in the first field
  • Enter the month you were born in the second field
  • Enter the year you were born in the third field
  • Click Submit and get alerted on your Akan name
